=== Design Decisions ===
- Silently skips notification if no project member exists (no error thrown)
- Notification failure does NOT roll back alert evaluation
- PM role_code='PM' preferred; falls back to first active member
- BizType='ALERT' for alert-triggered notifications
- NotifyType='INFO' (handled by NotificationService.create())
